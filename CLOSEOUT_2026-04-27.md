## 2. Profile Findings (verified from artifacts)

All numbers below cite exact files in `outputs/profile_gpu/`. The directory is gitignored; numbers are reproduced in this doc as the durable record.

### 2.1 Per-step wall-time decomposition

Source: `outputs/profile_gpu/phase_events.jsonl` (474 events, aggregated by `(step, phase)`).

| step | total (s) | collect (s) | collect % | evaluate (s) | backward (s) | optim_step (s) |
|------|-----------|-------------|-----------|--------------|--------------|----------------|
| 0    | 221.19    | 22.75       | 10.3 %    | 80.27        | 118.11       | 0.06           |
| 1    | 150.44    | 21.29       | 14.2 %    | 42.01        | 87.11        | 0.03           |
| 2    | 143.16    | 13.71       | 9.6 %     | 42.14        | 87.27        | 0.03           |

**Read:** Rollout (`collect`) is 9.6 %–14.2 % of step wall time, **not** the 70–90 % seen in LM-RL. The dominant cost is `evaluate + backward` (the diffusion ELBO grad computation), which is compute-bound.

### 2.2 GPU utilization per phase (sample-weighted)

Source: `outputs/profile_gpu/gpu_samples.csv` (7 516 samples × 100 ms ≈ 12 min wall) cross-joined with `phase_events.jsonl` by timestamp.

| phase      | runs | sample-weighted GPU util |
|------------|------|--------------------------|
| collect    | 3    | 69.8 %                   |
| evaluate   | 228  | 75.8 %                   |
| backward   | 228  | 89.4 %                   |
| optim_step | 3    | 100.0 %                  |

Aggregate over the whole 12-min run: mean GPU util 56.9 %, peak 100 % (`gpu_samples.csv`). The "low" mean is dominated by setup / model-load idle minutes — during the dense phases, util is at the 75-100 % bracket.

### 2.3 Memory pressure

Source: `outputs/profile_gpu/gpu_samples.csv` column 4 (`memory.used` in MiB).

- mean used: 28 455 MiB
- peak used: 29 505 MiB
- card capacity: 32 607 MiB
- **peak utilization: 90.5 %**

**Read:** No headroom for a second co-resident model on a single 5090. The "policy + reference + reward all on one GPU" mental model from LM-RL does not survive on diffusion at this resolution (416×240, 33 frames, num_steps=20).

### 2.4 Per-grad-step cost (K=4 `ppo_epochs`)

Source: same JSONL, computed as `(evaluate + backward + optim_step) / n_backward` per step.

| step | n_backward | grad_total (s) | per-grad-step (s) |
|------|------------|----------------|-------------------|
| 0    | 76         | 198.44         | 2.61              |
| 1    | 76         | 129.15         | 1.70              |
| 2    | 76         | 129.44         | 1.70              |

Steady-state per-grad-step ≈ **1.70 s**. Step 0 is inflated by torch.compile warmup (the user-reported "step 0/1 collect bubble" is also reflected in step 0's higher `collect` time).

### 2.5 Caveat on the "−49.5 % per-grad-step" claim

The sprint brief asserted "ppo_epochs=4 verified via wall-time profile: −49.5 % per-grad-step (71.1 s → 35.9 s)". **No K=1 baseline profile artifact is present in `outputs/profile_gpu/`** — only the K=4 run. The directional claim (K>1 amortizes the rollout cost across more grad steps and reduces per-grad wall) is consistent with the §2.1 data, but the exact 71.1 / 35.9 numbers cannot be reproduced from the artifacts in this sprint's directory. Flagged for owner: either re-run K=1 baseline and archive, or downgrade the wording in the PR.
